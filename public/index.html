Project Include

A non-profit initiative aiming to make coding accessible to all people!

Welcome to the future!

A future where every child regardless of who they are or where they come from has had the opportunity to learn programming in a Fun and Accessible way!

What We Do

We partner with schools and libraries in the GTA to deliver coding workshops and bootcamps!

Project Based

Our curriculum emphasizes learning by doing, and we send our students home with projects they have built to show their friends and family.

Workshops and Bootcamps

Our workshops introduce students to the world of Computer Science and Programming, and we also organize extended bootcamps where students leverage one or more programming languages to build projects.
